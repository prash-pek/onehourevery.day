One Hour Every Day

We live in a world that's always on.
1HED is your daily invitation to just...exist.

#1HED

It's pretty simple

One hour
No screens
Your way

In a world full of pressure to stay digitally connected,
give yourself a boredom break.

What can I do?

Watch clouds move
Actually taste your coffee
Notice your breathing
Pet your dog slowly
Draw something badly
Just sit there

What happens?

You get what you want from this hour

Questions you might have

What counts as tech?

Screens, mostly. But hey, your hour, your rules.

When should I do it?

Whenever works. Morning person? Evening explorer? Lunch break dreamer? It's all good.

What if I miss a day?

Tomorrow exists. No stress.

Ready to slow down?
